This image shows the first attempt at structuring the example web site looked at in this article. The component pages are organized in a spider diagram, with the “Home” page at the centre. the pages linked to this central point are “Store”, “Biographies”, “Fan Discussion”, “Tour dates and locations, “Pictures”, “Discography” and “Contact”. The “Discography page has two further sub-pages spidering off it, “Lyrics” and “Sample tracks”.
